Food: Chocolate Cake

This is a very quick and easy recipe for a chocolate cake. Try it out! :)
Recipe by chefkoch.de

    Ingredients:
  • 125g flour
  • 1 table spoon of baking powder
  • 180g sugar
  • 1 pack. of vanilla sugar
  • 120g butter
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 125ml water
  • 10g cocoa powder
  • 1 egg (medium)
  • powder sugar
  • chocolate chunks if desired
Preparation

Slowly heat up water, butter, sugar, vanillasugar, cocoapowder and chocolate together in a cooking pot.
Transfer into a mixing bowl and let cool for about 15-20min.
Then add flour, the egg d baking powder.
Pur into a cake pan and bake it at 170 degrees for about 40-45minutes.
Let cool and decorate with some powder sugar.
ENJOY!
